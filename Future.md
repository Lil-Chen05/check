# Future Features

Distant features planned for Check. These are not on the immediate roadmap.

## Ranked Mode

- **Skill tiers**: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum progression
- **Matchmaking**: Skill-based room matching for balanced ranked games
- **Separate stats**: Ranked wins/losses/points tracked independently from Casual
- **Season system**: Periodic resets with season-end rewards and historical records
- **Placement matches**: Initial calibration games to assign starting tier

## Social & Profiles

- **Public profile pages**: View any player's stats, tier, and recent game history
- **Friends list**: Add friends, see when they're online, invite to rooms
- **Match history**: Full log of past games with scores and outcomes

## Game Modes

- **Spectator mode**: Watch ongoing games without playing
- **Rematch / quick rematch**: One-click rematch with the same group after a game
- **Tournament brackets**: Organised in-app tournaments for a group of players

## Other

- **Replay system**: Watch back key moments from finished games
- **Achievements / badges**: Unlock cosmetic badges for milestones (first win, 100 games, etc.)
- **Notifications**: Alerts when friends come online or invite you to a game
